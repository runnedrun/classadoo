Classadoo offers instruction in two formats:

Classadoo at Home

Up to fifteen students around the world connect to a Classadoo teacher. Simply the best way to independently learn how to code. All you need is a laptop and internet access.

Classadoo at School

Students share a video conference and have their own software workspaces. Perfect for providing or supplementing Computer Science, Computer engineering or Design Thinking curriculum at schools, after school programs or homeschool groups.
